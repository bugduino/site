Some things you should know

In Lelylan we have a team culture. We encourage team members to do research. We have a formal research plan that includes incentive comp for conference presentations and a simple process to make sure team members get bench time and resources to complete research. All Lelylan employees get unlimited free books from Amazon. You see a book you want, you use your Lelylan account, you get the book, its yours, full stop.

We work with cutting edge tecnologies like Ruby, NodeJS, Test Driven Development, Heroku, Amazon Webservices, Redis, MongoDB, GIT, Javascript, HTML5, CSS3 and lot more. We do not use them just because they are 'hot' right now, but because they are the perfect tools to make what we need.

Our hiring process

Are you interested in Home automation? You can't waste our time. The first step is to get in touch with us. We're happy to talk about our field and what we do. Some of the best people we've worked with didn't have a formal background on what we are searching for, but they are geek, lovers and passionate. That is waht we ask first.

Our hiring/interviewing process goes roughly as follows. This is going to look complicated, but we're trying to overcommunicate. It can be stressful talking to a prospective new team! Here's what you can expect with us.

  1. Mailing and phone call - We'll get on the phone and talk to you about the company and what our work looks like. At the end of this call you should have a good idea of what we do, how our hiring process works, and answers to questions about Lelylan. Most importantly, you'll have a contact at Lelylan to talk with and bounce questions off of through the duration of our process.
  2. Passion - We innovate home automation. Most soluitons created within the last years feel old. It's not buzz when we say 'reThink your house'. We truly believe in this and if you don't, you can't join us. As you will not be forced to work in an office, we need to see what you have done. This is possible in different ways: website, linkedin profile, github account and most important your experiences. You do not need to be the best in your field, but we need people that want to become the best.
  3. Knowledge screening - We do 1-3 phone screens. You'll talk to a senior Lelylan team member who will ask you about your background and talk you through scenarios and concepts from our day-to-day work. It you are a frontend developer, you'll be talking about user interfaces. If you're a backend developer, you'll be talking about code. If you are a creative thinker, you'll be talking about social media. If you are a device integrator, you'll be talking about home automation standards and gateways. And so on, for every open position.
  4. Final meeting We've talked. We've done phone screens. We've answered questions. You've done challenges for us. At this point we both have a pretty good idea whether you'll be happy working with us. If that's the case, we'll bring you onsite (when possible) for an in-person interview, which concludes our hiring process.

How do I send my resume?

If you arrived till here, you must be interested enough to send us you resume, so write us a mail to jobs@lelylan.com completing this simple template.

  • Position - Front-End Developer, Back-End Developer, Devices Integrator, Creative Thinker, User Interaction Hacker, Data Analyst, Security Engineer and Energy Saver.
  • Linkedin profile - The link to your Linkedin.com profile. If you do not have one, you should seriously think about creating it right now.
  • Web site - The link to your website, blog, portfolio, github account or whatever can show us what you have been doing until now.
  • Motivations - We want to hear and feel why you want to join Lelylan. As we are a start-up we feel the need of people that is able to create and solve new challanges every day and that share all results with the world.
