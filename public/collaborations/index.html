Developers and web designers

Our main goal right now is to build a rock solid API. In the first period we will focus only on the backend and we'll be seeking for collaborations with developers and web designer to build creative mashups, intuitive user interfaces and smart services for your house.

If you have the knowledge and the interest on exploring what can be created with Lelylan check out the documentation in the developer center and start building something new and cool. You could even make some money on it.

Hardware suppliers

Lelylan develops services that access phisical devices using Unite Nodes. Even if we have all the information at hand to define Unite Nodes, a strong technological knowledge is needed to cover a specific protocol. For this reason it is important to collaborate with suppliers to cover the largest number of devices.

We also seek contact with the manufacturers of appliances, and in general, devices used inside the house to introduce a new way of thinking about the ecosystem of interoperable devices.

The advantages suppliers might have as the outcome of these collaborations, are that once their devices are covered, they can offer all services surrounding Lelylan as product to their customers. If you feel we can make something together, start learning more about building your first Unite Node.

Hackers and innovators

Lelylan wants to encourage users who share an affinity towards innovation. People with good technological background, a passion for domotics and open-source hardware (Arduino) could start hacking and provide feedback to make us build a better service. If you are one of those check out some tutorials and get your hands dirty!
