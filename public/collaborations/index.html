Hardware suppliers

Lelylan access phisical devices through Unite Nodes. Even if we have all the information to define Unite Nodes, a strong technological knowledge is needed to cover a specific protocol. For this reason it's important to find hackers and suppliers to cover the largest number of devices. Once a supplier connects their devices to Lelylan, the outcomes clearly consist in the ability to offer all services surrounding Lelylan as product to their customers.
