Access your house from the web

Lelylan is a platform based on a set of REST web services that enable the access to all home automation resources (devices, locations, people and more). What we aim to do is mapping every device to a unique URI which will provide control over it. When such a connection is done, technological limitations disappear and devices become identifiable only through their functionalities. Nothing more.

Every device communicate

To access all devices in your house we need gateways. We call them Unite Nodes and they allow Lelylan to communicate with a set of devices based on a specific standard (e.g Zigbee, Konnex, X10 and Lonworks). When devices are connected through a Unite Node, they are physically accessible from all services built on top of Lelylan and they can communicate to each other without the technological limitations we face today.

Let anyone innovate

Anyone can integrate new solutions. Possibilities are unlimited and still to be discovered. Home automation is a young sector and it needs you to discover its potential.

  • Web applications - HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript
  • Mobile - IPhone, IPad and Android
  • Social - Twitter, Facebook, Jabber, IM
  • Voice recognition - Google Voice, Ribbit and Twillio
  • Eye tracking - Smart eyeglasses
  • Augmented reality - Layar, Google Goggles, Wikitude
  • Pseudo natural language - Virtual Assistant and Twitter
  • Neural based interfaces - Emotiv, NeuroSky and Nia
  • Spatial motion interaction - Wii Controller and XBox Kinect
  • Smart surfaces - Smart tables and smart walls

The integrations with Lelylan are endless and they covers lot of fields, like energy saving, security and user experience. We want to build a smart house, where smart really means smart. Here some example of what we can do.

  • Rule engines - e.g. close the cooling system if the window in the same room is opened
  • Scenery systems - e.g. soft light could be the scenery, turning the intensity of lights down
  • Recommendation systems - e.g. suggest to use appliances when prices are cheaper
  • Scheduling systems - e.g.open the shutters in the bedroom every working day at 7am.

Introducing a new point of view

With Lelylan there is a new approach to the market, where we give everybody the ability to create new solutions. With Lelylan you do not start from the physical domotics systems. You start from a fake domotic system that anyone can create and play with and only when you feel satisfied you can connect to the physical world.

As final result we imagine you going to the shop close to your house, buying a light or any other device, going back home, plugging it and having it available through the web, using an URI. We envision a system that everybody can have, something simple to use and fast to install. The device will then be able to talk with the other devices in the house, becoming smarter and following your needs in a better way.
