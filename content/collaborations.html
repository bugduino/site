Hardware suppliers

Lelylan access phisical devices through Unite Nodes. Even if we have all the information at hand to define Unite Nodes, a strong technological knowledge is needed to cover a specific protocol. For this reason it's important to start collaborations with suppliers to cover the largest number of devices. We also seek manufacturers of appliances, and in general, anyone can build devices used into the house. The advantages suppliers might have as outcome from these collaborations, are that once their devices are covered, they can offer all services surrounding Lelylan as product to their customers.
